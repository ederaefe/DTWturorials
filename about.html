Our Story

About DTW Tutorials

Destined To Win (DTW) Tutorials is Nigeria's leading educational platform, dedicated to making high-quality exam preparation accessible to every student.

Our Mission

DTW Tutorials is an educational hub that supports students preparing for WASSCE (WAEC), NECO, GCE, JAMB, Post-UTME, and BECE (Junior WAEC) exams with free, comprehensive Mathematics and Physics video tutorials on YouTube.

We aim to encourage STEM study across West Africa, simplify complex equations, teach quick mental mathematics tricks, and motivate young minds to achieve excellent living and career breakthroughs.

Founded by Blessing Seun Chukwuka

As an educator and visionary, Blessing Seun Chukwuka established DTW Tutorials to bridge the gap between classroom teaching and student performance, helping thousands of young candidates confidently unlock their potential and gain admission.
